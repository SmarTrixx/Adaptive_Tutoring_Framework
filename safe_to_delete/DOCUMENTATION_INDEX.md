## Key Concepts Quick Ref

### Difficulty
- **Range**: 20% - 80%
- **Start**: 50%
- **Change per answer**: ¬±1% to ¬±5%
- **Display**: Shows current and previous (e.g., "52% (üìà +2%)")

### Engagement
- **Measured**: Response time, patterns, behavior
- **Tracked**: Per response, per session
- **Used by**: Adaptation policy engine

### Policy Engine
- **Window-based**: Q1-5 exploration, Q6-10 exploitation
- **Performance-based**: Accuracy determines direction
- **Damped**: Prevents wild difficulty swings
- **Momentum**: Rewards sustained good performance

### Adaptation
- **Visible**: In feedback modal after each answer
- **Real-time**: Happens immediately
- **Logged**: All decisions recorded
- **Analyzed**: Available for research
